So, who are these hall of famers?

Of the 23,000 MLB players, roughly around 5000 players are eligible. Players must have played 10 seasons of Major League Baseball and have been retired from for five full seasons to be considered eligible.

Further, of these 5000 eligible players, there are only 330 players that have been voted to the hall of fame. That is 6.6% of the eligible players end up making it through.
